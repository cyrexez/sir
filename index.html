Who we are?

SiR is a network of instructors made up of highly exceptional and talented college students to provide top notch tutorial services to enable kids to excel in their schools and communities. Basically, we offer opportunities to high achieving students in colleges by paying them for teaching younger students.

Our Work

SiR creates professions and a source of income for undergraduate and postgraduate students. We partner with organisations to provide our instructors with access to a worldwide network of mentors and advisors, and provide unique virtual and physical gathering opportunities so that thousands of college students can make an income while in their dorm rooms. Our instructors do not only teach, they also learn by earning information and key skills that will help them thrive as entrepreneurs or contributors to the economy of the twenty-first century.

Launched in 2021 officially, SiR has been in existence since 2017. Scholars In Residence (SiR) aims to empower students in Nigeria by providing assistance to undergraduate and post graduate college students that demonstrate exceptional academic skills to provide after school services to people and earn an income to become financially independent. SiR provides revenue for qualified undergraduates and postgraduates by paying them to tutor students remotely. The benefits SiR provides to undergraduates in addition to a source of revenue include:
  • Student Loans
  • Assistance of Postgraduate Application to Foreign Universities
  • Mentoring
  • Academic Advice
  • Paid Online Courses and Training
  • Job Placement Assistance and Networking
