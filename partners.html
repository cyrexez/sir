Gain Africa

GAIN is a worldwide network of Africans who believe in a better Africa resulting from a newly empowered generation of leaders. GAIN is out to change the game of leadership, education and investments in the society by leveraging on international and local connections with professionals in various sectors. GAIN’s mission is to inspire, nurture, mentor and empower the generation of future leaders; to empower every talented individual into actualizing their goals; and to create financial independence for most families and individuals.

Bookspals

Bookspals is a reading club that aims to bring children together to read, share books, reviews and swap stories and also earn rewards while at it. Thus, bringing together what a child might feel he/she has been obligated to lose by reading– fun and effective social interactions while increasing their intelligence, developing their minds and interests. Bookspals also aim at improving parents-child interaction and offer the opportunity of reading more books at less cost. Bookspals’ mission is to help children develop a reading culture and make them lifelong readers by engaging in activities and programs that are appropriate, conducive, and fun for children.

QPAT Solutions Limited

Qpat Solutions Limited propels students to achieve their goals and dreams whether helping a young student become an expert in reading skills, giving advice on securing admission to a higher institution; facilitating scholarship or assisting a graduate along the way to doing a Master’s degree. Basically, Qpat Solutions Limited facilitates and supports all the processes involved in actualizing her vision thereby creating an unequalled standard by building a network of talented people within Nigeria and Africa as a whole.
